Public Open Space

The public should be afforded full and fair access to beaches!

Choose an entry point to the trail:

A. TheSavin Rock information center and museumhas a large parking lot for residents. There is an exterior stairway and wheelchair-accessible inclined path that leads to the trail.

B. Bradley Point Park is near the beach with supervised swimming, boating, fishing and seasonal restrooms. Paid parking is nearby with a paved pathway connecting to the trail.

C. Old Grove Park has a handicap accessible pathway leading to the trail, free parking and seasonal restroom nearby. There are plenty of benches both in the park and along the trail.
